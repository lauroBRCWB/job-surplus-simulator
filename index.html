Assumptions & limitations
  • Balance-sheet, not forecast. Available workers vs required workers under your chosen output path.
  • Linear population decline to 2100; no age-structure dynamics.
  • Employed share compresses participation, unemployment, retirement, migration.
  • Productivity compounds for N years, then flat.
  • Demand matters. Output growth can absorb labor or amplify displacement.
